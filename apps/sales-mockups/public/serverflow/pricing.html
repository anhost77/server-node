Frequently Asked Questions

Do you host my servers?

No. ServerFlow is a deployment tool, not a hosting provider. You bring your own VPS (Hetzner, DigitalOcean, etc.) and we deploy your code to it.

Is it secure?

Yes. We use a zero-trust architecture. Our agent initiates outbound connections only. We never store SSH keys or have root access to your machine outside of the deployment agent.

What happens if I stop paying?

Your servers and apps remain online and fully functional. You just lose the ability to deploy new updates via ServerFlow until you resubscribe.
